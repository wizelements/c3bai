# Partner Tier Pricing - $65/Hour

## Overview

In addition to our standard tiering ($125/hour), we offer **Partner Rate pricing at $65/hour** for qualified partners, referrals, and established relationships.

## Who Qualifies?

Partner pricing is available to:
- **Referral partners** - You sent us business
- **Existing customers** - Long-term ongoing relationship
- **Partnership discussions** - We've had strategic conversations
- **Community members** - Active in our network
- **Early supporters** - Backed us when we were starting

## How to Qualify

1. **Fill out the inquiry form**
   - Complete all sections normally
   - Reach Section 6: "Do You Qualify for Our Partner Rate?"
   - Select your relationship type

2. **Provide details**
   - Who referred you?
   - What partnership did we discuss?
   - How do you know us?

3. **We validate**
   - We confirm your relationship
   - Approve partner status
   - Offer partner pricing in quote

## What's Included in Partner Tiers

Partner tiers include the same deliverables as standard tiers:
- Same engineering hours
- Same token allowances
- Same support level
- Same features & integrations

**The only difference:** 48% discount on hourly rate ($65 vs. $125)

## T&C

- Partner pricing must be validated before contract
- Pricing valid for contract duration (typically 12 months)
- Early exit fee: 25% of remaining term (same as standard tiers)
- Annual prepayment discount: 10-15% additional (same as standard)
- Partner status confirmed in writing before engagement begins

## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch to partner pricing mid-contract?**
A: No. Partner pricing is determined at contract signing. If your status changes (e.g., you become a referral partner), we can renegotiate at renewal.

**Q: Does partner pricing include different deliverables?**
A: No. Deliverables, support, and features are identical. Only the hourly rate changes.

**Q: How long is partner pricing valid?**
A: Partner pricing is locked in for your contract term (typically 12 months). At renewal, we reassess qualification.

**Q: Can I get partner pricing without filling out the form?**
A: Contact us directly at partnerships@c3bai.com if you want to discuss partner status before submitting the inquiry form.

**Q: What if I don't qualify now but think I should?**
A: No problem. We can revisit at renewal or if circumstances change (e.g., you refer a customer, we discuss a partnership later).
