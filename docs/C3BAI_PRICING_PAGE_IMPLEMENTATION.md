## PART 3: FAQ (Answer Real Objections)

### "How much will my project cost?"

**Answer**: It depends on scope. We size projects based on:
- Token volume (how much inference?)
- Data processing (how many documents?)
- Customization (how much custom logic?)
- Timeline (fast sprint or long build?)

**Example**: "A booking system like Beltline Golf = 124 hours = fits Starter tier for 6 months"

Use the inquiry form to get specifics.

### "Why do you charge a setup fee?"

**Answer**: The setup fee covers real work:
- 20-40 hours of our time (consultation, integration, testing, docs)
- Infrastructure setup (dashboards, monitoring, APIs)
- Security audit (compliance, authentication, permissions)
- Onboarding training (your team learns the system)

Not "activating your account" (which takes 5 minutes). Real engineering.

### "What if I go over the included tokens?"

**Answer**: 
- **Transparent pricing**: You see overage costs before they happen
- **Annual cap**: Can't exceed cap without explicit approval
- **Optimization**: We proactively find ways to reduce usage (usually saves money)
- **Example**: Starter tier at 80% cap triggers cost optimization review

### "Can I get a discount?"

**Answer**:
- **Annual prepay**: 10-15% off if you pay upfront
- **Multi-seat**: Volume discounts at 3+ concurrent seats
- **Long-term**: 20-30% discounts for 3+ year commitments (Enterprise)
- **Not**: Never discount base rate. You're getting market-fair pricing already.

### "What if I only need help for a month or two?"

**Answer**: Two options:
1. **Pick a tier, commit 12 months** (with 25% early exit option if needed)
2. **Hourly consulting** ($125/hr) if under 20 hours/month

Most find the monthly retainer better (predictable, no surprises).

### "How do I know you'll actually deliver?"

**Answer**: 
- Look at our portfolio projects (below)
- All documented with real code, real results
- 100% success rate on 5+ completed projects
- First month is low-risk (25% exit fee is tiny if disappointed)

### "Do you work with [technology X]?"

**Answer**: Yes. We're technology-agnostic:
- Any LLM provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, local models)
- Any stack (Next.js, Python, Go, Rust, etc.)
- Any data source (databases, APIs, files, streams)

Tell us your tech stack in the inquiry form.

### "What's your SLA? What if you miss a deadline?"

**Answer**:
- **Starter/Professional**: Best-effort, no SLA
- **Enterprise**: Custom SLA negotiated (typically 99.9% uptime)
- **Communication**: We communicate delays immediately
- **Remediation**: If we miss deadlines, we credit hours back
