## Recommended Pricing Structure

**"Free Feasibility Analysis"**
- Free AI project assessment
- ~30-45 min consultation
- Deliverable: Feasibility report with complexity & investment estimate
- Purpose: Qualify leads, build pipeline
- **Implementation**: Contact form → scheduling → deliverable (PDF/video)

---

- SLA guarantees

**$15,000+/month (custom)**

## Risk Mitigation

### Price Too High?
**Risk**: Low conversion rate  
**Mitigation**: Start with free consultations, track willingness to pay

### Price Too Low?
**Risk**: Unsustainable, low-quality customers  
**Mitigation**: Monthly commitment minimum ($2,500), quality filter via consultation

### Token Overages?
**Risk**: Unexpected costs, customer churn  
**Mitigation**: Monthly cap alerts, cost optimization consulting included

### Support Overhead?
**Risk**: Support costs exceed margin  
**Mitigation**: Tiered support SLAs, automated monitoring & escalation

## Best Practices Applied

✅ **Value-Based Pricing**: Tiers reflect value (capacity, support, SLAs)  
✅ **Transparent Pricing**: Clear breakdown of what's included  
✅ **Freemium + Paid**: Free consultation funnels to paid tiers  
✅ **Usage-Based Component**: Overage model aligns costs with value  
✅ **Anchoring**: High Enterprise price anchors perception of Starter  
✅ **Annual Discounts**: Encourages commitment, improves CAC payback  
✅ **Consumption Transparency**: Tokens, docs, calls clearly tracked  
✅ **Upsell Path**: Starter → Professional → Enterprise (clear progression)  
✅ **SLA Differentiation**: Support/uptime justify premium pricing  
✅ **Add-on Revenue**: Overages create expansion revenue
